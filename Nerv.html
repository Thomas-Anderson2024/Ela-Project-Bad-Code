NERV

  • No one ever reaches vitality. Vitality means good health, a state of being strong and active. The NERV group that causes the events of Fahrenheit 451 does not believe humans should be able to reach this, no one should reach strong and healthy thinking, instead, they make them dumb, and the opposite of vitality.
  • Originally founded in order to keep society from collapsing, time has ruined these intentions and now complete control of knowledge is desired.
  • The organization originated as a final line between humanity and blasphemy, but as time progressed leaders became obsessed with world ruling and escalated their boundaries.
  • The general populous becomes weak, feeble minded, dumb, and ignorant. Similar to god, NERV almost fears what humans are capable of with books.
