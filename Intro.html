Introduction

  • Our prequel is founded off of the idea that a religious organization, NERV, has achieved control over the flow of knowledge. By taking over the digital space, NERV has begun brainwashing humans to become uninspired, lackluster, and dumb.
  • NERV now needs control over physical knowledge. This all stemmed from one professor. In the pursuit of knowledge, he has discovered blasphemous power.
  • As more books were made public, knowledge begins to stack exponentially. The professor doing independant research believes that what he found should be forbidden; Immortality and omnipotence.
  • He attempts to burn his work, but this does not solve the issue, so using religious reasoning the professor gathers a large following.
  • As time progresses, the group changes leadership and corrupts, NERV starts to control the flow of information throughout the world, mainly by burning the information.
