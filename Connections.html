Connections

  • Burning Babel connects to Farenheit 451 because in our prequel, Burning Babel, NERV is burning books and scrambling humans’ minds through the usage of technology making them dumber and more ignorant, preventing them to learn about immortality and “blasphemous” knowledge (knowledge going against god).
  • In Fahrenheit 451, the firefighters continue to burn books and the houses that have books inside. Their world is also consumed by technology feeding them mindless information to keep them ignorant and thoughtless. Seldom are aware that a religious organization are behind this, due to the brainwashing.
  • Very few people, such as Faber and Montag, are suspicious that there is a motive behind the burning of books, but are not sure who are truly behind it.
